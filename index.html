ABOUT

This is a simple web-based list app that used to track:

  • Games that you own but haven't played yet - your backlog.
  • Games that you don't own but want to get - your wishlist.

Click on "Backlog" or "Wishlist" to swap between both lists.

To add a game to either list, enter the details of the game into the fields, then click "Add Game". A game will only be added if there is a name present in the field.

To remove a game from either list, click the entry's "Delete" button in the list.

To save your data, click "Save Data".

To clear your lists completely, click "Clear Data".

To close out of this window, click anywhere.
